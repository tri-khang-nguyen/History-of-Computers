Signifigance to Computer Science

In 1976, Steve Jobs founded Apple Computer Inc. with Steve Wozniak. In 1979, Steve led a small group of engineer’s and discovered that by using a Graphical User Interface, he can make computers easier to use and more efficient. In the early 1980s, Steve began to engineer the Macintosh, or Mac as the computer became known. This was the first model that the Macbooks which we see today are derived from. At first, the computer’s were underpowered and expensive, and when Jobs failed to properly address the issue he was removed from Apple in 1985.
