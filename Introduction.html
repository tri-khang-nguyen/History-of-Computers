Introduction

Born: February 24, 1955
Place of Birth: San Francisco, California, United States
Died: October 5, 2011
Known For: Co-Founder of Apple, Co-Founder of Pixar Studios, being a major pioneer in the creation of personal computers.
Steve Jobs grew up in Silicon Valley, California, and was raised by adoptive parents. As a kid, he was always interested in en gineering and computers. He went to Reed college in Portland, Oregon, but dropped out and took a job as a video game designer in 1974, which was his first experience professionally working with computers. Later in the year, he left that job and connected with Steve Wozniak, a former high school friend, who he would eventually co-found Apple Computers with.
